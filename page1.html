PAGE ONE: BOUQUET

The first thing you see is a bouquet that looks exactly like the one from that one perfect night. Soft petals, a slow bloom, a ribbon the color of champagne. The animation loops like a memory you refuse to stop replaying.

“This rose knows my secrets but not my name,” the caption whispers under the image, then repeats with errors:
This r0se kn0ws my s3crets but n0t my n4me.
this rose knows my secrets but not my nnnnn_ _ _

The bouquet invites you deeper into the garden, into the archive of the night.
